Headteacher's Welcome

Stourfield Junior School is a large community school with over 500 pupils and 60 staff. Our aim is that all the children at our school grow into responsible, caring members of society who are confident, independent, lifelong learners. 

We are a tremendously nurturing and inclusive school and believe that pupil performance and well-being go hand in hand.
